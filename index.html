FAQ

Questions we hear on every call.

Do I need to replace or migrate away from my current helpdesk?
+
No. Marvin sits on top of your existing helpdesk — it doesn't replace it. Your team keeps using Zendesk, Intercom, HubSpot, or Freshdesk exactly as they do today. Marvin plugs in as an additional AI layer. No data migration, no workflow changes.
How long does setup take and who does it?
+
We handle the entire setup. Once you give us access to your helpdesk and share your KB, Marvin is typically live within a few hours. You don't need a developer.
What happens when Marvin doesn't know the answer?
+
Marvin escalates — with full context, not a dead end. Your agent receives the full conversation, the customer's intent, matched articles from the KB, and a drafted response where possible. Your team never has to ask the customer to repeat themselves. And importantly, how your team responds trains Marvin for next time.
What if Marvin starts sending wrong answers to our customers?
+
You set the confidence threshold. Marvin only sends autonomous answers above that threshold — everything else comes to your team first. For sensitive topics, you can configure guardrails that permanently route certain categories directly to a human, regardless of confidence. You're always in control of what reaches your customers.
How is Marvin different from the AI that's already built into my helpdesk?
+
Three core differences. First, Marvin learns — it updates its knowledge base from every escalation your team handles. Bundled AI trains once and stays static. Second, Marvin escalates with context — full conversation, intent, and a draft, not just a ticket number. Third, Marvin works across all your channels — not just inside one helpdesk's interface.
